DMX Scene Example

Dimmer Channels

We assume simple dimmers from 1 to 4

We assume RGB + dimmer: e.G. Starting at channel 5 (R), 6 (G), 7 (B) and dimmer at (8)

So we end up with dimmers at channels: [1,2,3,4,8]

Other Channels

Other channels, for example 'RGB' or color-wheel channels also need to be set, however you probably do not want to 'fade them in'.

For example 'fading in' a pan or rotation channel together with the dimmer, would move your light as it comes up
